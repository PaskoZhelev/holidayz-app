# Holidayz App

📅 Stay Ahead with Global Public Holidays & Regional Days Off! 🎉

Looking for a simple and reliable way to check public holidays worldwide?

🌍 Public Holidays Calendar provides a complete overview of national and regional holidays for 100+ countries, all in an easy-to-use calendar format.

### Key Features:
- ✅ View public holidays for over 100 countries
- ✅ Check regional holidays for specific areas
- ✅ User-friendly calendar layout for quick browsing
- ✅ Plan ahead with accurate holiday information
- ✅ Stay updated with yearly holiday schedules

Whether you're planning a vacation, managing work schedules, or just staying informed, this app helps you keep track of important holidays worldwide. Download now and never miss a holiday again! 🎊🎈
